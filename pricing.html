Website Monitoring

You'll be notified by email or text message whenever your site is down or unreachable, making it easy for you to get it up and running as soon as possible. Track Your Site's Loading Speed

The SEO ToolBox allows you to automatically monitor your site's loading speed. That means you'll be able to pinpoint when your server is having speed issues – and correct this. Again, this will result in more traffic for your site.
